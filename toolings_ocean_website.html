Who We Are

Toolings Ocean is a Bangalore-based precision engineering firm established in 2013, under the proprietorship of Smt. Sudha R. Chavan.

Registered as a Small Scale Industry (Regn. No. 290201118412), we specialise in plastic injection moulding, tool and die making, and precision job works — serving clients across defence, aerospace, automobile, and electronics sectors.
