How We Work

Tool Manufacturing
Process

From raw steel to approved first samples — our in-house toolroom covers every step of precision mould and press tool manufacturing.

01 — DESIGN
📐
CAD/CAM Design
3D mould design with parting surface analysis, gate and runner layout, cooling channel routing, and DFM review before machining release.
02 — MACHINING
⚙️
CNC & Grinding
CNC milling of core-cavity blocks, precision drilling, and surface grinding to achieve flatness within microns for perfect mould shut-off.
03 — EDM
⚡
EDM Sparking
Electrical discharge machining for sharp internal ribs, fine textures, and complex cavity forms impossible to achieve by cutting tools alone.
04 — FITTING
🔩
Assembly & Lapping
Core-cavity assembly with ejector system, cooling connections, and guide pillars. Hand lapping of parting surfaces for zero flash.
05 — TRIAL
🔬
Mould Trial
First article injection trial on our in-house machines. Dimensional inspection of moulded samples. Corrections until approved parts are achieved.
06 — DELIVERY
✅
QC & Dispatch
Full dimensional report, surface finish check, and protective packaging. Approved samples and inspection records dispatched with the tool.

Engineering Excellence

What Sets Our
Tooling Apart

Every tool built at Toolings Ocean is engineered for repeatability, long service life, and ease of maintenance. Our experienced toolmakers ensure quality at every stage.

Tolerance to ±0.01mmCNC machining and surface grinding routinely achieve dimensional tolerances of ±0.01mm for critical mould and die features.
P20 / H13 Tool Steel StandardPre-hardened P20 for medium volumes and heat-treated H13 for high-volume production tooling exceeding 500,000 shots.
Optimised Cooling DesignCooling channel layout designed to reduce cycle time and eliminate warpage and sink marks on moulded components.
In-House Mould TrialsAll moulds are trialled in-house on our injection moulding machines before delivery. Approved first samples provided.
Prototype to Mass ProductionAluminium prototype tooling for early validation. Full hardened steel production tooling for 100,000+ shot production life.
